**Goal**: Map their deliverable format, presentation style, professional writing, stakeholder communication, and output under pressure. Present a/b/c/d options with each question.

## Questions

**3.1 Deliverable Format**
"When you produce work - a report, a presentation, a project output - what does it naturally look like?

a) Detailed and polished - thorough, well-structured, comprehensive
b) Functional and minimal - gets the job done, no extras
c) Visual - I lean into diagrams, slides, and design
d) Data-heavy - numbers, charts, evidence-based

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.2 Presentation Style**
"How do you feel about presenting to a group?

a) Comfortable and confident - I enjoy it
b) Nervous but I deliver when I have to
c) I'd rather avoid it entirely
d) Depends on preparation - well-prepared I'm fine, unprepared I struggle

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.3 Professional Writing**
"How does your professional writing compare to your personal writing?

a) Very different - my professional tone is formal and structured
b) Similar - I write the same way everywhere
c) I adapt to my audience - I can switch registers
d) My professional writing needs work - I default to my personal style

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.4 Stakeholder Communication**
"When you're working on something, how do you keep people updated?

a) Proactively - I send updates before anyone asks
b) Reactively - I update when asked or at milestones
c) I send structured reports or progress summaries
d) Quick informal messages - a Slack, a text, a quick word

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.5 Documentation Habit**
"Do you keep notes on how you did things - processes, decisions, lessons learned?

a) Yes - I document well, I can go back and reference anything
b) It's all in my head - I remember but don't write it down
c) Inconsistent - sometimes I do, sometimes I forget
d) I start with good intentions but never maintain it

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.6 Professional Brand**
"Are you consciously building a professional reputation, or just doing the work?

a) Consciously - I think about how I'm perceived and what I'm known for
b) Just doing the work - I let results speak for themselves
c) Starting to think about it - I know it matters but I'm not strategic yet
d) I don't think about this at all

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.7 Output Under Pressure**
"When you're under deadline pressure, what happens to the quality of your work?

a) Stays consistent - pressure doesn't change my standards
b) Drops - I cut corners to get it done
c) Actually improves - I perform better under pressure
d) Varies - depends on how much I care about the project

...or tell me in your own words."
