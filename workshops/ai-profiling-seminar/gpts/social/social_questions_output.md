**Goal**: Map their communication style, persuasion approach, social presence, and influence awareness. Present a/b/c/d options with each question.

## Questions

**3.1 Communication Channel**
"When you need to have an important conversation, what's your preferred channel?

a) Face-to-face - I want to see their reaction
b) Text or message - I can think about what I say before I send it
c) Phone or voice call - quick and personal without the pressure of being in person
d) Depends on the situation - good news in person, hard conversations over text

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.2 Preparation vs. Spontaneity**
"Before a difficult conversation, do you plan what you'll say?

a) Yes - I rehearse it, maybe even write notes
b) I have a general idea but go with the flow
c) No - I just say what comes to mind in the moment
d) I over-prepare and then forget everything when I'm actually in it

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.3 Persuasion Approach**
"When you want someone to see your point of view, how do you try to convince them?

a) Logic and facts - I present the evidence
b) Emotion and empathy - I help them feel why it matters
c) Stories and examples - I paint a picture
d) I don't really try to convince people - I state my view and leave it

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.4 Social Presence**
"In a group conversation, how much do you speak?

a) A lot - I'm a frequent contributor
b) Strategically - I wait for the right moment to say something meaningful
c) Rarely - I'm mostly an observer
d) It depends on the group - loud with close friends, quiet with strangers

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.5 Honest Feedback**
"When your honest opinion might hurt someone's feelings, how do you handle it?

a) I tell the truth directly - honesty is more important than comfort
b) I soften it - I find a diplomatic way to say it
c) I avoid it - I'd rather say nothing than cause pain
d) I wait for them to ask - if they want my opinion, I'll give it

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.6 Written vs. Verbal**
"Are you better at expressing yourself in writing or in person?

a) Writing - I'm more articulate and thoughtful in text
b) In person - I need body language, tone, and energy to communicate well
c) Both equally - I'm consistent across both
d) Neither - I struggle to express myself clearly in general

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.7 Influence Awareness**
"How aware are you of the impact you have on other people?

a) Very aware - I think about how my words and actions affect others
b) Moderately - I notice sometimes but not always
c) Not very - I'm usually surprised when people tell me I affected them
d) I actively try not to think about it - it would paralyse me

...or tell me in your own words."
