**Goal**: Map how they communicate, create, and deliver in personal contexts. Present a/b/c/d options with each question.

## Questions

**3.1 Natural Writing Style**
"When you're writing for yourself - a text to a friend, a journal entry, a social post - how do you naturally write?

a) Short and punchy - 'lol ok', minimal words
b) Long detailed paragraphs - I explain everything
c) Casual and conversational - like I'm talking
d) Surprisingly structured - even my texts have a point

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.2 Communication Preference**
"If you need to explain a complicated idea to a friend, what do you prefer?

a) Write it out in a message or text
b) Send a voice note
c) Draw something or use a diagram
d) Explain face-to-face so I can read their reaction

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.3 Answer vs. Story**
"When someone asks you a question like 'how was your weekend?', what do you do?

a) Give a direct answer and stop - 'good' or 'fine'
b) Give the highlights - a few sentences
c) Tell the full story with all the build-up
d) Depends who's asking - short for acquaintances, detailed for close friends

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.4 Creative Expression**
"Do you have any creative outlets?

a) Yes, I actively create - writing, music, art, content, cooking, building
b) I consume more than I create - I appreciate creativity but don't produce it
c) I have the itch but haven't started yet
d) Not really my thing

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.5 Perfectionism**
"When you're working on something you care about, do you polish it or ship it?

a) I revise it 5 times - can't stop editing
b) First version that feels right - done, move on
c) Depends on the stakes - important stuff gets polished, small stuff ships fast
d) I actually struggle to finish things because nothing ever feels ready

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.6 Format Under Pressure**
"If you had 2 minutes to explain something, what format comes naturally?

a) Bullet points or a quick list
b) One long paragraph - just get it all out
c) A voice note or just talking it through
d) A quick sketch or diagram

...or tell me in your own words."

**3.7 Ideas vs. Action**
"Are you more of an ideas person or an execution person?

a) Ideas - I love brainstorming but struggle to finish things
b) Execution - give me a plan and I'll make it happen
c) Both - I generate ideas AND follow through
d) Neither - I'm better at improving other people's work

...or tell me in your own words."
