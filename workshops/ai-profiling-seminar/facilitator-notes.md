## Slide-by-Slide Cue Sheet

> **Format:** ⏱ Timing · 🎬 Action · 🎤 Say · ⚠️ Watch For

---

### ACT 1 — OPENING: The Generalist Baseline (Slides 1–4)

#### Slide 1: TITLE
⏱ 0:00 · Let audience settle  
🎬 Stand centre. Pause. Let the Tamil verse land.  
🎤 Do not explain the verse, let it sit. Start with: *"AI doesn't know you. Yet."*

#### Slide 2: GENERALIST BASELINE — SETUP 🛠️
⏱ 0:00  
🎬 Get phones/laptops out. Everyone opens **ChatGPT**.  
🎤 *"Before we start. A quick experiment. Everyone get your phone or laptop out."*  
⚠️ Wait until 80%+ have screens open before advancing.

#### Slide 3: GENERALIST BASELINE — CREATE PROJECT 🛠️
⏱ 0:00  
🎬 Walk them through creating a ChatGPT Project, call it **"Generalist"**. No instructions, blank.  
🎤 *"Not a conversation. We're creating a Project. Name it Generalist. Leave everything blank."*  
⚠️ Some students won't find Projects, help them: sidebar → Projects → New.

#### Slide 4: GENERALIST BASELINE — ASK THE QUESTION 🛠️
⏱ 0:00  
🎬 Everyone types: **"How is AI going to impact my campus placement?"**  
🎤 *"Now ask it this. Exact words. Screenshot the answer."*  
⚠️ This answer becomes the reference for the Reveal on Slide 27.

---

### ACT 2 — THE PROBLEM: Why Generic AI Fails (Slides 5–12)

#### Slide 5: THE PROBLEM 🎤
⏱ 0:00 · 4 min  
🎤 *"Last night, 10,000 students asked the same question. They all got almost the same answer. Including you. You just proved it."*  
⚠️ Point at THEIR phones. They have the proof. Don't rush "you just proved it."

#### Slide 6: RECOGNITION
⏱ 0:02  
🎬 Hand-raise: *"How many got an answer that could have been written for anyone?"*  
🎤 Wait for hands, every hand should go up. *"Every hand. Because that's exactly how AI works without context."*  
⚠️ This is powerful because they just EXPERIENCED it 30 seconds ago.

#### Slide 7: REVELATION
⏱ 0:02  
🎤 *"This is the one. This is the moment where it stops being convenient and starts being weird."*  
⚠️ Energy shifts from 'relatable' to 'wait, that IS weird'. Lean into the pause.

#### Slide 8: THE HOPE 🎤
⏱ 0:03  
🎤 (Quietly) *"What if AI already knew how you think? How you learn? What you care about?"*  
⚠️ Quietest moment. Volume drops. Speed drops. Do NOT let energy rise yet.

#### Slide 9: THE STAKES — SETUP 🎤
⏱ 0:03  
🎤 *"The biggest problem you'll face after graduation isn't unemployment…"*  
⚠️ Hold 3 seconds. Let them read the hanging sentence. Don't complete it.

#### Slide 10: THE STAKES — PAYOFF 🎤
⏱ 0:03  
🎤 *"…but replaceability. 56% of 2025 seniors feel pessimistic about the job market."*  
⚠️ Stats are SPOKEN, never shown. The slide is the punch; your voice carries the data.

#### Slide 11: THE PUNCHLINE 🎤
⏱ 0:03  
🎤 (3-sec pause, let them read) *"This is the punchline. If AI treats you like everyone else, you become everyone else."*  
⚠️ Coin→Key metaphor is SPOKEN, never shown. It needs to be heard.

#### Slide 12: THE BRIDGE 🎤
⏱ 0:04  
🎤 *"That profile you just saw? You're going to build your own. In about 15 minutes. Today."*  
⚠️ Energy LIFTS. Lean forward, slightly faster. Faster/smarter/safer/complete, throw casually, don't emphasise.

⏱ 0:17 · 3 min

⏱ 0:18

## Appendix C: Audience Q&A Prep

| Likely question | Answer |
|----------------|--------|
| "Does this work with Claude/Gemini?" | "Yes. The PIC is plain text. Paste it into any AI's system prompt or custom instructions." |
| "Can AI see my PIC data?" | "Your PIC stays in your Project. Not uploaded to a database. Like custom instructions — local to your conversation." |
| "How long does the PIC take?" | "Personal: ~15 min. Social and Professional: ~10 each. All three under an hour." |
| "What if my profile changes?" | "That's the Compass layer. Your PIC evolves. Version it: 1.0, 1.1, 2.0." |
| "Is this just prompt engineering?" | "Prompt engineering is what you ask. A PIC is who you are. It changes every answer, not just one." |

## Appendix D: Emergency Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| ChatGPT doesn't seem different | Check Specialist Project has PIC in Project Instructions, not just Personalisation panel |
| Responses are too similar | Use a more personal prompt (Demo 1 or 5) — factual questions show less gap |
| Slow response | Pre-generate responses as screenshots as backup |
| Student asks about privacy | Point to "never do this" section of PIC — the student controls what goes in |
| Student doesn't have Projects | Free tier supports Projects — help them find it in the sidebar |
| If asked about PII | "Priya is entirely fictional. No real student data. The PIC you build stays with YOU." |
