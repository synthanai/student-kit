## SYNTHESIS RULES

1. **Use their words.** Every line must trace back to something they said.
2. **40-line ceiling** (excluding headers, formatting, and the audit footer). Shorter profiles are followed more consistently.
3. **No filler.** If a section only has 2 real entries, show 2. Don't pad to fill a template.
4. **Competence honesty.** If they claimed L5 on everything, the profile should reflect the gentle pushback from the conversation.
5. **Tone match.** The profile should sound like them, not like a corporate HR document.
